About Us

Team bi0s from Amrita University is a well-established group of computer security enthusiasts focusing on various areas including but not limited to web application security, computer forensics, binary exploitation, reverse engineering, cryptography and Android security.

bi0s is largely a CTF team actively taking part in the international CTFs round the year besides carrying out research on security issues in the real world. Fuelled by the urge for community service, bi0s has been organizing workshops and educating the masses about the need for cyber security.

Achievements

First team from India, formed in 2007. bi0s was invited to compete in PHDays CTF, Russia. Conducts InCTF, Exclusively for Indian students. So far, has conducted 6 editions. bi0s was ranked 68th globally in Google CTF 2016 amongst 2500 teams Finished CSAW 2016 finals at 2nd position

At present

Avinash Joshi in 2M, Varun Ramani, Reno Robert and Akshay Krishnan in VMware, Anirudh Anand in Flipkart and others in DRDO.
